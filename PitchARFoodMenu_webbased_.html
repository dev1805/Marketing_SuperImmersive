Here are some insights into the Development Process

To ensure the successful implementation of the AR food menu, we propose the following development process:

  1. Initial Consultation:
  • We will engage in a thorough discussion with you to understand your specific requirements, goals, and desired features for the AR food menu.
  1. Prototype Development:
  • Based on the initial consultation, we will develop a prototype that showcases the key functionalities and interactions of the AR food menu.
  • We will use one or two menu item images as image markers and create realistic 3D models of the meals through food scanning techniques.
  1. Demo and Feedback:
  • We will provide the prototype for testing and feedback.
  • Your input will be crucial in refining the interactions and making necessary changes to ensure the AR food menu aligns with your expectations.
  1. Full Development:
  • Once the prototype is approved, we will proceed with the full development of the AR food menu, incorporating the refined interactions and features.
  • We will create AR content for all menu items, ensuring a consistent and immersive experience throughout the menu.
  1. Work in Progress:
  • Throughout the development process, we will keep you informed about the progress, sharing work-in-progress versions of the AR food menu for your review and feedback.
  • This iterative approach allows for continuous collaboration and ensures that the final product meets your requirements.
  1. Publication:
  • Upon completion, we will deliver the final AR food menu for deployment in your establishment.
  • Our team will assist with the necessary setup and integration to ensure a smooth transition to using the AR food menu.
